Opportunities

We are excited to announce various opportunities within our research group. We invite motivated individuals to apply for PhD and postdoctoral positions, as well as undergraduate research opportunities. Below are the details of the available positions and funding options.

Postdoctoral Opportunities

We invite applications for postdoctoral positions. Candidates are encouraged to seek external funding, with support available for crafting competitive proposals. Below are some potential funding sources:

Available Scholarships for Postdoctoral Researchers:

  • International Research Fellowship Program (Deadline in November 25th)
  • Innovative Solutions Grant (Deadline April 1st)
  • Interdisciplinary Research Fellowship

PhD Positions for 202X/202X

We are seeking enthusiastic PhD candidates to explore innovative applications of artificial intelligence in chemistry. Interested applicants should submit their CV and a brief statement of research interests to the lead researcher. Please note that funding will primarily be available for candidates classified as “Home” students.

Available Scholarships for PhD Students:

  • National Scholarship Program
  • Global Chemistry Fellowship
  • University Merit Scholarship

Undergraduate Research Opportunities

Our lab is open to hosting undergraduate students for research projects related to computational methods in chemistry. Interested students pursuing degrees in chemistry, physics, or related fields should contact us to explore available opportunities. Bursaries may be available through departmental initiatives.
